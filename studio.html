What it does

Your archive, finally askable.

Producers sit on hundreds of hours of recorded conversation — and the best moments are effectively lost in their own drives. Fuchsia Studio changes that.

Search everything you've recorded

Ask your archive in plain language and land on the exact spoken moment — segment-highlighted, quote-accurate, the real voice on the right words within a heartbeat.

On-device and private

Recording and transcription happen entirely on your Mac with Apple's on-device speech. Your audio never leaves the machine. Intimate material stays where it belongs.

From search to assembly

Pull found moments into word-accurate clips, shape a structure, draft narration, record your real takes, and watch the episode come together — the app is the edit surface.

An intention multiplier, not an author

AI woven through the work amplifies your intent — it scaffolds and suggests, never imposes. Any synthetic voice is your own (Personal Voice), only ever a preview your real performance replaces. It never clones a presenter or a subject.

Who it's for

Built for documentary and podcast producers.

From intimate, interview-driven pieces to complex, multi-speaker, multi-episode work — where your material is your craft, and your trust in it is everything.
