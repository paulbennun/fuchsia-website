Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

The short version

Fuchsia Studio is built privacy-first. Your audio never leaves your Mac. Recording and transcription happen entirely on your device. There is no account to create, and we do not run servers that collect your recordings, transcripts, or personal information. The only data that ever leaves your Mac is transcript text — and only when you use the optional AI assistance, sent to the model provider you have configured.

This policy explains how Fuchsia Studio ("the app", "we", "us") handles your information. Fuchsia Studio is a macOS application for documentary and podcast producers, published by Fuchsia, LLC.

1. Our approach: privacy by design

Fuchsia Studio works with intimate material — people's interviews, memories, and voices. We treat that material as something to be protected, not harvested. Wherever a feature can run on your device, it does. The app's privacy boundaries are enforced by how it is built, not just promised in this document.

2. Information the app accesses

Fuchsia Studio works with files and recordings that you choose and that stay under your control. Specifically:

Your audio, recordings, and media

You grant the app access to a folder (your archive) that you select. Your source audio and any takes you record stay on your Mac, inside the locations you choose. Audio is never uploaded to us or to any third party.

Transcripts and derived data

The app transcribes your audio on-device using Apple's built-in Speech framework, and builds a private, searchable index (transcripts, timecodes, speaker labels, and search embeddings) so you can find moments in your own material. This derived data is stored locally alongside your project.

Microphone

With your permission, the app uses your microphone to record narration and takes in your own voice. These recordings are saved to your project on your device.

Optional AI assistance

By default, Fuchsia Studio runs entirely on your device — no AI service is contacted unless you explicitly enable and configure one. If you turn on AI assistance (for example, to suggest structure, draft narration, or answer questions about your material), only transcript text is sent to the provider you choose — never your audio. With AI assistance off, nothing leaves your Mac.

No account, no tracking

Fuchsia Studio does not require you to create an account. We do not use advertising, and we do not include third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not build profiles of you or sell data — we don't have it to sell.

3. How information is used

Information is used only to provide the app's features to you: transcribing your audio, making it searchable, helping you assemble shows, and — if you choose — providing AI assistance. We do not use your content to train models, and we do not use it for advertising.

4. Where your data is stored

Your recordings, transcripts, and project data are stored locally on your Mac, in the folders you choose. They are protected by your device's own security (such as FileVault and your macOS user account). Backups are under your control — for example, Time Machine or any backup service you use. Because your data lives on your device, deleting it is entirely in your hands.

5. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We do not share your content with third parties for their own purposes. The only outbound transmission the app makes is transcript text to the AI model provider you configure, for the AI features you choose to use.

6. Third-party services

Fuchsia Studio relies on Apple's on-device frameworks (such as Speech and Natural Language), which process your data on your Mac. If you enable AI assistance, transcript text is processed by the model provider you configure; that provider's handling of that text is governed by its own terms and privacy policy. Choose a provider you trust.

7. Your rights and choices

  • Permissions: You can grant or revoke microphone, speech recognition, and file access at any time in macOS System Settings → Privacy & Security.
  • Access and deletion: Because your data is stored locally in folders you control, you can view, export, or delete it directly. Deleting your project files, and the app, removes your data from your device.
  • AI features: You can choose not to use AI assistance, keeping all processing on-device.
  • Regional rights: Depending on where you live (for example, under GDPR or the CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Because we do not collect or hold your personal data on our servers, these rights are largely exercised directly on your device; contact us if you have questions.

8. Children's privacy

Fuchsia Studio is a professional tool intended for adults and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, provide a more prominent notice.

10. Contact us

If you have any questions about this policy or your privacy, email us at support@fuchsiaprojects.com.
